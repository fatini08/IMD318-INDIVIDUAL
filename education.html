My Education Journey

SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN TOH JOHAN

I completed my primary education at Sekolah Kebangsaan Toh Johan , where I gained a strong foundation in subjects such as Bahasa Melayu, English, Mathematics, Science and Bahasa Arab. During my time at the school, I was actively involved in various co-curricular activities, including sports and clubs which is Netball club and Puteri Islam club that helped me develop important skills like teamwork and leadership.

SABK MAAHAD AL-KHAIR LIL BANAT

I continued my journey at Sabk Maahad Al Khair Lil Banat. This is where I discovered my passion for DINI subject which is Lughatul Arabiah,Usul Al-Din and Al- Syariah,and participated actively in Netball Club and representing the school in Majlis Sukan Sekolah Daerah(MSSD). I also complete my SPM examination here and got (3 A) for subject Bahasa Melayu, Sejarah and Perniagaan.

UNIVERSITI TEKNOLOGI MARA

Currently, I am pursuing my studies at UiTM Kedah, where I major in Information Management. University life has been an enriching experience, full of learning opportunities and personal growth. I am proud to have achieved a CGPA of 3.2, reflecting my dedication and consistent effort in my academic journey.
